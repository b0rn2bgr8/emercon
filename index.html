ABOUT US

Emerald management consulting was established in the year 2008 to provide professional services in the areas of construction project management, management contracting and environmental management and positioned to deliver projects to the satisfaction of our clients. Since its inception the company has been built from the ground up to provide excellent services for the community, government, public and private sector. Based in Johannesburg, Emerald management consulting has successfully managed projects and provided services in South Africa and a number of African countries. Our team of professionals consists of specialists in different aspects of the Built Environment professions. Passion for quality and the highest ethical standards are our goal.
